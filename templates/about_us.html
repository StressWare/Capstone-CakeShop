Our Story

How It All Began

Our story began in 2014 as a simple home baking hobby, but it wasn't until 2020 that it truly transformed into a business. When the pandemic hit, we were working from home and looking for a way to secure our family's future amidst the uncertainty.

It all sparked when a former nursing classmate requested a cake. That single order encouraged us to open our kitchen to friends and family. As word-of-mouth spread, an officemate suggested we create a public page, opening our doors to a wider community.

To keep up with the growing love for our creations, we enrolled in professional baking and decorating classes to elevate our craft. Today, we are proud to have grown from a local home-based kitchen in Iloilo City into a thriving cake shop that accepts international transactions and ships our sweet treats worldwide.

The Person Behind It All

Meet The Baker

Mr. and Mrs. Brave
Founder & Head Baker

Mr. and Mrs. Brave are the dynamic duo behind the brand. Mrs. Brave, the Head Baker and creative mind, discovered her passion for baking in 2014 when she started baking for her daughter, Xai. Her love for the craft deepened during the pandemic when she started baking monthly celebration cakes for her newborn, Xia.

Driven by a desire to perfect her art and better serve her customers, she continuously upgrades her skills through advanced baking and cake decorating classes, ensuring every cake is not just delicious, but beautifully designed.

Mr. Brave is the backbone of the operation. His foresight, alongside a suggestion from an officemate, was instrumental in expanding the business beyond their immediate circle. Together, they took a leap of faith during the lockdown to turn a comforting hobby into a sustainable livelihood.

What started as a personal gesture for their daughter has blossomed into a homegrown cake shop in Iloilo City, turning their passion into purpose, one sweet celebration at a time.
