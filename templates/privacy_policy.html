Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or place an order, Mrs. Brave's Cake Shop collects the following:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Username
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Order history
  • Login activity

We only collect information that is necessary for processing your orders and managing your account

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to process and deliver your orders, manage your account, and improve our service. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in during your visit.

4. Third Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and account management, Google reCAPTCHA v3 to protect our website from abuse, and Google Gemini to power our AI chat feature. Chat conversations may be logged for service improvement purposes. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Payments are processed through PayMongo. We do not store your payment details. Please refer to PayMongo's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Your Rights Under RA 10173

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct — update inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete — request erasure of your personal data
  • Object — object to the processing of your data
  • Data Portability — request a copy of your personal data in a portable electronic format

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Order records may be kept for up to 3 years for legal and business purposes. Upon account deletion, your personal data will be anonymized or removed.

7. Data Breach

In the event of a personal data breach, Mrs. Brave's Cake Shop will notify affected users and the National Privacy Commission (NPC) within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).
